Operator Workflow

From preparation to post-operation review.

01

Prepare

Confirm equipment, batteries, tools and site requirements.

02

Inspect

Complete aircraft, propeller, structure and waterproofing checks.

03

Connect

Set up the ground station, telemetry, hose and pressure washer.

04

Operate

Conduct controlled flight, cleaning or façade inspection.

05

Shut Down

Land, disarm, isolate pressure and disconnect system power.

06

Review

Inspect the drone, record faults and review CrackScan data.

Important Safety Notice

Training does not replace authorised supervision.

Project SkyScrub should only be operated by trained personnel under an approved operating plan. Complete all pre-flight checks, establish a controlled operating zone and follow applicable aviation, electrical and site-safety requirements.

Need technical support?

Refer to the operating manual and troubleshooting procedures before contacting the Project SkyScrub team.
