The problem

Traditional façade cleaning is slow, expensive, and exposes workers to height risk.

Height Risk

Rope access and scaffolding place workers in hazardous environments during façade maintenance.

Setup Time

Conventional methods require manpower, staging, permits, and long preparation windows.

Limited Data

Cleaning and inspection are often separated, making defect tracking slower and less connected.

Our solution

One drone platform. Cleaning, inspection, and reporting in a single workflow.

SkyScrub uses a hexacopter platform connected to a ground-based pressure washer. The drone carries the nozzle, sensors, waterproofing shell, and CrackScan AI system while water is supplied from the ground to reduce onboard mass.
