The Problem

Traditional façade cleaning is risky, slow, and labour-intensive.

Height Risk

Rope access and scaffolding place workers in hazardous high-rise environments.

Setup Time

Conventional methods require manpower, staging, permits, and long preparation windows.

Limited Data

Cleaning and inspection are often separated, making defect tracking slower.

The Solution

One drone platform. Cleaning, inspection, and reporting in a single workflow.

Ground-fed Cleaning

Water is supplied from the ground to reduce onboard drone mass.

Waterproof Protection

Protected electronics support operations around cleaning mist.

AI Inspection

CrackScan records crack evidence for future reporting.

Prototype

SkyScrub Mark I.

The first integrated prototype developed to validate high-pressure façade cleaning, waterproofing, structural integrity, and AI-assisted inspection.

Next Generation

SkyScrub Mark II.

Improved Endurance

Cleaner packaging and optimised weight distribution.

Modular Payloads

Better access to sensors, nozzle, and electronics.

LiDAR Support

Future-ready altitude and obstacle sensing.

Software

CrackScan.

AI-powered crack detection software that stores inspection records with screenshots, timestamps, and location fields.
