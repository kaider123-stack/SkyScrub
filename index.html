The Problem

Traditional façade cleaning is risky, slow, and labour-intensive.

Conventional façade maintenance often depends on scaffolding, rope access, or gondolas. These methods increase setup time, manpower dependency, and worker exposure to height-related risks.

Worker Safety

Reduces reliance on manual work at height.

Setup Efficiency

Minimises the need for heavy temporary access systems.

Inspection Data

Combines cleaning with AI-assisted defect records.

Ground-fed Cleaning

Water is supplied from the ground to reduce onboard mass.

Waterproof Protection

Electronics are protected from mist and cleaning spray.

AI Inspection

CrackScan supports crack detection and record keeping.

The Solution

One platform. Cleaning, inspection, and documentation.

SkyScrub uses a hexacopter platform carrying the nozzle, sensors, waterproofing enclosure, and CrackScan system while the pressure washer remains ground-based.
